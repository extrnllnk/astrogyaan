About Us

AstroGyaan is an AI-powered astrology and palmistry platform created to make ancient wisdom accessible, affordable, and easy to use for everyone. Whether you're a job seeker seeking career clarity, a working professional looking for personal guidance, a business owner exploring favorable times, or someone from a rural background searching for genuine spiritual support—we’re here for you. Through a blend of cutting-edge AI and trusted astrological knowledge, AstroGyaan offers personalized insights in Kundli creation, marriage compatibility (Kundli Milan), palm reading, and live consultations. That’s why AstroGyaan brings spiritual guidance directly to your fingertips—anytime, anywhere.
